ExcelMind College - Site Plan

ExcelMind College

This name represents a digital academic portal for ExcelMind College, providing resources, admissions info, and updates to students, parents, and staff.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the ExcelMind College is to serve as an academic and informational platform for current and prospective students of ExcelMind School. The site will provide access to academic resources, admission information, events, and announcements.

Scenarios

  • How can I apply for admission to ExcelMind College?
  • Where can I access academic resources or the school calendar?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #f8f9fa - used for page background and light areas.

Secondary Color: #141b41 - used for headers, navigation, and main text color.

Accent Color 1: #306bac - used for section titles and UI highlights.

Accent Color 2: #4562f2 - used for buttons and call-to-action highlights.

Accent Color 3: #98b9f2 - used for subtle background highlights and borders.

Typography

  • Headings: "Poppins", sans-serif - for all major section and page headings.
  • Body Text: "Lato", sans-serif - used for readable paragraphs and content.
  • Accent Text / UI: "Montserrat", sans-serif - used for buttons and interface labels.
